# V3.4 Matchup-First Overlay

- Preserves the full V3.3 individual hitter score.
- Final score = 75% individual model + 25% matchup attackability.
- Matchup attackability = 70% opposing starter vulnerability + 30% park/weather.
- Adds offense-specific matchup grades.
- Adds Matchup_Hitter_Rank and Matchup_Cluster_Pick.
- Adds a Matchup Clusters tab and Excel worksheet.
- Marks the top two hitters only when the offense's attackability score is at least 60.
- Does not force two hitters from every game.
- No V4 or V5 calibration logic is included.
