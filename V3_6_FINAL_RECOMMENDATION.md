and a difficult opposing pitcher.

## Why column

The Why column displays up to four primary reasons, including:

- Recent hot contact
- Best or elite pitch fit
- Platoon advantage
- Switch-hitter status
- Piñata or difficult pitcher/game
- Park upgrade
- Weather boost or downgrade

The existing V3.5 model score, hot symbols, matchup score, and platoon markers
are unchanged. This patch changes presentation and recommendation hierarchy,
not the underlying score.
